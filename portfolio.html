About Me

Hello, My name is Yagmur Guzeldereli. I was born on April 20th 1995, in Istanbul, Turkey. I've lived in America for 14 years now. I feel more American than I do Turkish. Its nice to visit time to time to see different cultures between the two countries. I think thats what made me who I am. Ive been learning something new everyday, from a new word to new activities. I wanted to learn coding because I like the idea behind it. To make a website, or a game or to be able to create something with never ending possibilities.
